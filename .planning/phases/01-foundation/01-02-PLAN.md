Key narwhals differences from polars:
- `nw.LazyFrame` has NO `.cast()` frame-level method — use `lf.with_columns(nw.col(key).cast(dtype))`
- `nw.LazyFrame` has NO `.cast({key: type})` dict syntax either
- `nw.to_native(obj, pass_through=True)` is required (without pass_through=True raises TypeError on native frames)
- narwhals exceptions: `nw.exceptions.InvalidOperationError`, `nw.exceptions.ComputeError` (from `narwhals.stable.v1.exceptions`)
