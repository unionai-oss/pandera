<objective>
Eliminate the module-string sniffing in `_concat_failure_cases` (ARCH-02) and fix the silent-drop bug where scalar polars failure frames are dropped when PySpark frames are present. Approach: change `_build_lazy_failure_case` to return narwhals-wrapped frames (drop the `nw.to_native(enriched)` call); then dispatch in `_concat_failure_cases` on `nw.Implementation` instead of module strings; convert polars scalar frames to a compatible representation (or surface the limitation as a clear error) so they are not silently dropped.

Purpose: Remove the second of four `is_pyspark` dispatch violations and fix the genuine bug where COLUMN_NOT_IN_DATAFRAME / dtype-mismatch failure cases were lost in PySpark error reports.

Output: Updated `_build_lazy_failure_case` and `_concat_failure_cases`, plus any required adjustments to `failure_cases_metadata` to handle narwhals-wrapped failure-case items.
</objective>
